# Exam Family Phase 0-1 Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist prevents us from calling an exam family "ready" just because a preset pack exists.

Phase 0-1 means the family has explicit product definition and family-aware authoring coverage.

It does not automatically mean:

- advanced scoring is complete
- analytics are family-mature
- mobile and device QA are complete
- long-tail question types are fully proven

## Checklist Legend

- `Done`: implemented and validated in product or automation
- `Partial`: present but still needs hardening
- `Pending`: not yet implemented or not yet proven
