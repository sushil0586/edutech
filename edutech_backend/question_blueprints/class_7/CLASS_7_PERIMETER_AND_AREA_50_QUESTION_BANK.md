## Question 1
Question Type: Direct Concept
Difficulty Level: Foundation
Question Text: What does perimeter measure?
Options:
- A. the space inside a figure
- B. the distance around a figure
- C. the number of corners
- D. the height of a figure
Correct Answer: B
Explanation: Perimeter is the total length of the boundary of a figure. The space inside a figure is measured by area, not perimeter.
Common Mistake: Confusing area and perimeter because both are measured from shapes.
Learning Objective: Distinguish perimeter from area.
