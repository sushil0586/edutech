## Question 1
Question Type: Direct Concept
Difficulty Level: Foundation
Question Text: Which pair of lines never meet, even when extended on both sides?
Options:
- A. Intersecting lines
- B. Perpendicular lines
- C. Parallel lines
- D. Transversal lines
Correct Answer: C
Explanation: Parallel lines remain the same distance apart and never intersect, even when extended endlessly. Intersecting lines meet at one point, perpendicular lines are a special kind of intersecting lines, and a transversal is a line that crosses other lines.
Common Mistake: Thinking perpendicular lines do not meet because they look separate before extension.
Learning Objective: Identify parallel lines correctly.

## Question 2
Question Type: Direct Concept
Difficulty Level: Foundation
Question Text: What is the measure of a right angle?
Options:
- A. `45 degrees`
- B. `90 degrees`
- C. `180 degrees`
- D. `360 degrees`
Correct Answer: B
Explanation: A right angle measures exactly `90 degrees`. `45 degrees` is acute, `180 degrees` is straight, and `360 degrees` is a complete angle.
Common Mistake: Confusing a straight angle with a right angle.
Learning Objective: Recall the standard measure of a right angle.

## Question 9
