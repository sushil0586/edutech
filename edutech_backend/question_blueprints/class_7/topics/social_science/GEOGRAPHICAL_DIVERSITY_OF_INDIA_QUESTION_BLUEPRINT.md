# Geographical Diversity of India

- Class: 7
- Subject: Social Science
- Topic code: `SST-GEO-DIVERSITY`

## Scope

- mountains, plains, plateaus, deserts, coasts, climate links, and regional diversity

## Premium Question Lanes

- region-feature matching
- map or description interpretation
- compare how landforms affect life
- misconception correction on uniform geography

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only “identify the landform” recall
- rotate settlement, crops, travel, climate, and resources contexts
- advanced should link geography to human life
- include comparison and inference

## Incremental Build Plan

- benchmark 10 across major regions
- expand 25 with human-life connections
- scale after representation balance review
