# Environment and Human Interaction

- Class: 7
- Subject: Social Science
- Topic code: `SST-GEO-ENVIRONMENT`

## Scope

- how people adapt to and affect environment, resources, and sustainability

## Premium Question Lanes

- cause-effect reasoning
- best-action environmental decisions
- compare two human-environment situations
- local resource and responsibility cases

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only slogan-style environment questions
- vary forest, water, city, village, mountain, and coastal contexts
- advanced should require trade-off reasoning
- distractors should reflect common oversimplifications

## Incremental Build Plan

- benchmark 10 with adaptation + impact mix
- expand 25 with case-based prompts
- scale after context audit
