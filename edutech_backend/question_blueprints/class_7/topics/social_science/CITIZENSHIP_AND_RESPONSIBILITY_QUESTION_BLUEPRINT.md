# Citizenship and Responsibility

- Class: 7
- Subject: Social Science
- Topic code: `SST-CIVICS-CITIZENSHIP`

## Scope

- duties, participation, respect for rules, and everyday responsible citizenship

## Premium Question Lanes

- practical decision scenarios
- compare responsible and irresponsible actions
- school-to-community behavior links
- public-good reasoning

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only “good citizen” slogans
- vary traffic, cleanliness, voting awareness, school property, and community help
- advanced should require judgement, not moral recall
- use realistic conflicting options

## Incremental Build Plan

- benchmark 10 with action-based prompts
- expand 25 with responsibility trade-offs
- scale after nuance audit
