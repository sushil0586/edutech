# Division of Fractions

- Class: 7
- Subject: Math
- Topic code: `MATH-FRACTIONS-DIVIDE`

## Scope

- division meaning, reciprocal logic, and sharing / grouping contexts
- interpretation of fraction division in practical settings
- avoid treating invert-and-multiply as the only learning goal

## Premium Question Lanes

- choose correct division interpretation
- solve and justify reciprocal use
- identify the wrong step in a worked solution
- compare division result size with intuition

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only invert-and-multiply procedural items
- include grouping and measurement contexts
- advanced must ask why the operation works or what it means
- distractors should mirror reciprocal confusion

## Incremental Build Plan

- benchmark 10 with meaning-first items
- expand 25 with worked-step errors
- scale after context balance review
