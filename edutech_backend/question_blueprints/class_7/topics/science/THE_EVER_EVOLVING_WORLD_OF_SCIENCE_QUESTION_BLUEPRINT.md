# The Ever-Evolving World of Science

- Class: 7
- Subject: Science
- Topic code: `SCI-EXPLORATION-WORLD`

## Scope

- observation, evidence, curiosity, and change in scientific understanding
- examples from everyday life and simple discoveries
- avoid turning it into history-of-science memorization only

## Premium Question Lanes

- observation-to-inference
- identifying scientific attitudes
- best-method and evidence questions
- misconception correction about science being fixed

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only definition recall
- vary classroom, home, nature, and simple experiment contexts
- advanced should test reasoning about evidence
- avoid same “scientist observes” opening repeatedly

## Incremental Build Plan

- benchmark 10 with 6 stem families
- expand 25 with evidence-based cases
- scale after checking conceptual spread
