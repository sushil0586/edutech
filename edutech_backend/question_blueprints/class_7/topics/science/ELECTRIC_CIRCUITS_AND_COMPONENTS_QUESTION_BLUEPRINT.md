# Electric Circuits and Components

- Class: 7
- Subject: Science
- Topic code: `SCI-PHYSICS-ELECTRICITY`

## Scope

- simple circuits, cells, bulbs, switches, conductors, and safe use
- diagram reading at middle-school level

## Premium Question Lanes

- open / closed circuit reasoning
- identify missing or faulty component
- choose correct conductor or setup
- safety-based decision questions

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only “will the bulb glow?” clones
- vary diagrams, descriptions, and household contexts
- advanced should involve diagnosis or correction
- include plausible wiring mistakes as distractors
