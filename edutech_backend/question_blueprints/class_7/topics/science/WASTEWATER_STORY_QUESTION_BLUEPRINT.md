# Wastewater Story

- Class: 7
- Subject: Science
- Topic code: `SCI-ENV-WASTEWATER`

## Scope

- wastewater sources and sanitation basics
- drainage, treatment stages, sludge, and safe disposal at school level
- pollution prevention, hygiene, and community responsibility

## Premium Question Lanes

- sequence treatment steps
- choose best hygiene or disposal action
- infer outcome of poor sanitation practice
- compare safe and unsafe wastewater handling situations

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only “what is wastewater?” recall items
- do not repeat one sewage-treatment ordering shell endlessly
- vary home, school, street, village, and city sanitation contexts
- advanced should ask prevention and public-health reasoning, not only definitions

## Incremental Build Plan

- benchmark 10 with treatment and hygiene balance
- expand 25 with scenario-based public-health reasoning
- scale after duplicate-context review
