# Heat Transfer in Nature

- Class: 7
- Subject: Science
- Topic code: `SCI-PHYSICS-HEAT`

## Scope

- conduction, convection, radiation, insulation, and daily-life heat transfer
- simple natural and household situations

## Premium Question Lanes

- identify heat-transfer mode
- best-material or design reasoning
- explanation of natural phenomena
- compare two situations with different heat flow

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not ask only “which mode of heat transfer” recall
- vary utensils, sea breeze, clothing, animals, houses, sunlight
- advanced should require mechanism reasoning
- distractors should reflect mixed-mode confusion
