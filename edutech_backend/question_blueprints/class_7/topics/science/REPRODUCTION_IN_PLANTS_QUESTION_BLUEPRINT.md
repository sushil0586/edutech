# Reproduction in Plants

- Class: 7
- Subject: Science
- Topic code: `SCI-LIFE-PLANT-REPRODUCTION`

## Scope

- modes of reproduction in plants
- vegetative propagation, buds, seeds, spores, and simple examples
- pollination, fertilization, fruit and seed formation
- seed dispersal through familiar school-level contexts

## Premium Question Lanes

- classify reproduction mode from example
- sequence or process reasoning
- compare two reproduction methods
- infer why a dispersal method suits a plant

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only match-the-plant-to-method questions
- do not overuse mango / coconut / rose examples only
- vary flower, seed, spore, cutting, and dispersal contexts
- advanced should ask why a method helps survival or spread

## Incremental Build Plan

- benchmark 10 with method diversity
- expand 25 with process and comparison reasoning
- scale after visible-example duplication review
