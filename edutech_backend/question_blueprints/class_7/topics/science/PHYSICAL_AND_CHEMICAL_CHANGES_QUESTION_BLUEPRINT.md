## Scope

- reversible and irreversible changes, indicators of chemical change, and real examples
- avoid advanced reaction-equation detail

## Premium Question Lanes

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create endless classify-the-change lists
- include cooking, rusting, melting, dissolving, burning, and curdling contexts
- advanced should ask why, not only what
- vary visible clues: gas, heat, color, permanence
