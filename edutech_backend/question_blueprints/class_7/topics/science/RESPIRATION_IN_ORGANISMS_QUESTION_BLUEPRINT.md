# Respiration in Organisms

- Class: 7
- Subject: Science
- Topic code: `SCI-LIFE-RESPIRATION`

## Scope

- breathing and respiration distinction
- aerobic and anaerobic respiration basics
- respiration in humans, animals, and plants at school level
- breathing rate, gas exchange basics, and daily-life observations

## Premium Question Lanes

- distinguish breathing from respiration
- compare respiration across organisms
- observation or activity interpretation
- reason from exercise, rest, and environmental context

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not repeat only “breathing is not respiration” recall items
- do not create endless nostril / lung one-liners
- vary athlete, fish, insect, earthworm, and plant contexts
- advanced should ask why breathing rate changes, not only what happens
