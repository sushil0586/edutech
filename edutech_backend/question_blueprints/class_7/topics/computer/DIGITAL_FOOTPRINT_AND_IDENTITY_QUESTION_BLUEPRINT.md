# Digital Footprint and Identity

- Class: 7
- Subject: Computer
- Topic code: `COMP-SAFETY-FOOTPRINT`

## Scope

- online identity, personal information, sharing decisions, and lasting digital traces

## Premium Question Lanes

- safe-sharing decisions
- identify risky profile behavior
- compare strong and weak online identity habits
- long-term consequence reasoning

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only “never share password” items
- include photos, usernames, profiles, comments, and app use contexts
- advanced should involve judgement about partial information sharing
- avoid fear-only messaging

## Incremental Build Plan

- benchmark 10 with realistic child-safe scenarios
- expand 25 with consequence-based items
- scale after nuance audit
