# Cyber Safety and Wellbeing

- Class: 7
- Subject: Computer
- Topic code: `COMP-SAFETY-CYBER`

## Scope

- passwords, phishing basics, screen habits, respectful online behavior, and digital wellbeing

## Premium Question Lanes

- identify risky messages or actions
- choose safer response
- compare healthy and unhealthy digital habits
- support-seeking and reporting scenarios

## Anti-Repetition Rules

- do not create only password-strength recall
- vary chat, gaming, email, link-clicking, and screen-time contexts
- advanced should involve judgement under pressure
- include wellbeing, not only security

## Incremental Build Plan

- benchmark 10 with safety + wellbeing mix
- expand 25 with realistic threat scenarios
- scale after context spread review
